1. Ingest Securely

Connect Dorado to your local folders or SharePoint. It indexes your PDFs, Excels, and Word docs locally using MarkItDown. No data is uploaded to OpenAI.

2. Agentic Writing

Specialized "Worker Agents" (Llama 3.2) draft answers based only on your verified history. It cites the specific document and page number for every claim.

3. Compliance Guard

A "Critic Agent" (Qwen 2.5) reviews every drafted answer against your RFP's compliance matrix. It blocks hallucinations before you ever see them.
